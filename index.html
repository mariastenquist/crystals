Crystals Collector!

Game Instructions:

You will be shown a random generated number. Below that: there are 4 crystals.
Click on a crystal to add points to your score. Continue clicking on crystal(s) to attempt to match your score with the generated number given. You lose if your added points do not add up to the generated number. Each crystal possesses specific points, you must click on each to uncover its point value!
Good luck!
